How It Works

1. Sign Up

Create a free account and complete your profile.

2. Find or Offer a Ride

Search for rides that match your destination and schedule or offer a ride by providing your trip details.

3. Book and Pay

Book a ride and pay securely through our platform. Or accept ride requests as a driver and receive payments.

4. Enjoy the Journey

Travel together, save on costs, and make new friends while helping the environment.

About Pura Vida Rides

Pura Vida Rides is a carpooling service that connects drivers with empty seats to passengers looking for a ride in Costa Rica. Our mission is to provide a more sustainable, affordable, and social way of traveling.

Safety and Trust

Verified Profiles

All users are required to verify their identity and provide a photo. We also encourage users to link their social media accounts for increased trust.

Reviews and Ratings

After each ride, drivers and passengers rate and review each other. This helps maintain a positive and respectful community.

Secure Payments

Payments are processed securely through our platform, and funds are only released to the driver once the trip is completed.
